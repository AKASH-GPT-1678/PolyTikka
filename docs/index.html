Aryan Khan is all Set for its Directorial Debut

Aryan Khan is all set to make a bold and stylish directorial debut with his Netflix series, The Ba***ds of Bollywood. Produced by Red Chillies Entertainment and featuring a high-octane ensemble cast, the show is described as an unabashed, multi-genre ride through the glitzy yet gritty underbelly of the Hindi film industry. Coming to Netflix in mid-September 2025, it promises dark comedy, sharp satire, and unforgettable cameos.
