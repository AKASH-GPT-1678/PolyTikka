April 16 , 2004

Eknath Shinde Sparks Shiv Sena Split Rebellion Over Uddhav’s Alliance with Congress-NCP Forms Govt with BJP, Claims Legacy of Bal Thackeray

Eknath Shinde led a major rebellion within Shiv Sena, opposing Uddhav Thackeray's alliance with Congress and NCP. He claimed the party had drifted away from Bal Thackeray's original Hindutva ideology. Shinde later formed a new government with the BJP and was recognized as the official Shiv Sena leader.
